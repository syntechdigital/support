The Disaster & Its Impact

War has devastated communities, destroyed homes, and torn families apart. Children are growing up without access to food, education, or healthcare. Vulnerable families urgently need support to survive and rebuild their lives.

Your donation will go directly toward providing food, shelter, and medical care to those most in need. Every contribution, no matter the size, brings hope.

Problem / مشكلة

Families in Iran and across the region are suffering deeply from the ongoing war. Homes are destroyed, livelihoods lost, and children grow up without food, education, or healthcare. Vulnerable families are left with nothing but hope for help.

Ailə faciəsi – insanlar evsiz, ac və köməksizdir. (Azeri: Families face tragedy – homeless, hungry, and helpless.)

Cause / السبب

The struggle between Iran and Israel is both ideological and strategic. Iran opposes Israel’s existence and expands influence through proxies. Israel fights to block Iran’s nuclear ambitions and regional dominance.

Recent direct attacks have escalated the confrontation, making the situation far more dangerous. Innocent families are caught in the crossfire of this geopolitical struggle.

الحرب لا ترحم الأبرياء (War shows no mercy to the innocent.)

Solution / حل

Together, we can provide food, shelter, and medical care to those most in need. Every donation, no matter the size, brings hope and relief to families devastated by war.

Ümid paylaşmaq – həyatları xilas etməkdir. (Azeri: Sharing hope means saving lives.)

العطاء هو طريق السلام (Arabic: Giving is the path to peace.)
